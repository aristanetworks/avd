```eos

   net 49.0001.1000.7000.0010.00
   is-type level-1-2

   log-adjacency-changes

   timers local-convergence-delay 15000 protected-prefixes
   advertise passive-only

   address-family ipv4 unicast
      maximum-paths 4
      fast-reroute ti-lfa mode link-protection
   !

      maximum-paths 4
      fast-reroute ti-lfa mode link-protection
   !

      no shutdown
```
